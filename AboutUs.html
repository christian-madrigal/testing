ABOUT

Madrigal is a young and dynamic Chicago-based art gallery. We want to promote our emerging and mid-career artists in major markets around the nation. Madrigal wants to offer contemporary art in a relaxed and approachable atmosphere, so you can now enjoy visiting our 100% online gallery from your home or everywhere else! We just want to provide a fun and exciting art selection for you to gain access, increase your knowledge, fall in love and... buy art! We believe art is a gift to be shared, given and received with appreciation and respect. Share your passion, spread our art.
